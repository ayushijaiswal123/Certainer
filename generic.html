Breaking the common notion

Correlation with other fields

Social science deals with the social life of groups and individuals involving anthropology, economics, education, psychology, politicalscience, and social study.Social sciences are subjective and observational. Similarly, astrology is observational relating the position and motion of celestial objects to human character and situation. Astrology extends the span of social science to celestial objects. Social science like astrology uses statistical analysis.

Astrology and Quantum Mechanics

Astrology and quantum mechanics: Like quantum mechanics, astrology is also based on probability, not certainty. The uncertainty principle applies to astrology as well. There is some uncertainty ΔE of the energy particles reaching Earth. Similarly there is some uncertainty ΔΑ of the assigned attributes. ΔE. ΔΑ = astrological error Wave-particle duality applies equally well for the cosmic energies, because the same particles and energies are in astrology as they are in cosmology. The attributes of an electron is contextual, because they depend on the environment and the observing person. The contextual attributes in quantum mechanics apply to astrology well. The effectiveness of astrology prediction depends on the environment and observers. The same could be said about astrology. Astrology is not certainty but probability. Astrology is based on the data collected thousands of years ago. Since then it has not been updated.

Astrology explained by Cosmic energies and biofields

Astrology studies the effect of celestial bodies on humans and earthly affairs. Astronomy, cosmology, and physics study the celestial bodies and energies in the universe and their interaction between them. There should be no conflict between them. Simply because a phenomenon can not be explained, it does not mean it does not exist. The causes of gravitational force, electron charge, conductivity, magnetism , etc. are not known, but their effects are known. Science calls them property of matters. Similarly, the effect of celestial bodies on humans are known, but the causes are not known. Astrology is based on statistical analysis. Astrology is social science. Astrology is linked with alternative medicine. Astrology is explained by cosmic energies and biofield. The basis of astrology is similar to quantum mechanics. These five good reasons to justify astrology as a science – social science – should convince the readers and scientific community.
